Classic Homemade Pizza

A delicious pizza with a crispy crust and your favorite toppings

Description

Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world. It consists of a round, flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It is baked until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and golden.

This homemade version is simple, customizable, and perfect for beginners. You can add your favorite toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or vegetables.

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 2½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the toppings

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni (optional)
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • Bell peppers (optional)
  • Olives (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, then knead into dough.
  3. Let dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
  5. Roll out dough into a circle.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  7. Add cheese and your chosen toppings.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  9. Slice and serve hot.
