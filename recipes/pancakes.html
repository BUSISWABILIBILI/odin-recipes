Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast cakes made from a simple batter of flour, milk, eggs, and baking powder. They are quick to prepare and perfect for a sweet breakfast or brunch.

This recipe produces light and fluffy pancakes that can be served with syrup, fruit, butter, or any toppings you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 3½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan to form small circles.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip.
  7. Cook until both sides are golden brown.
  8. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of your choice.
